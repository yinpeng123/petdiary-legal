隐私政策

最近更新：2026 年 7 月 21 日

PetDiary（宠物日记，以下简称"本应用"）是一款帮助您记录宠物日常生活的 iOS 应用。我们非常重视您的隐私，本政策说明本应用如何处理您的信息。

一、核心原则：数据只属于您

  • 本应用没有服务器、没有账号体系、没有任何网络请求。
  • 您创建的所有内容（宠物档案、日记、照片、疫苗、体重、支出、提醒记录）仅存储在您设备的本地存储中，不会上传到任何服务器，我们也无法访问。
  • 本应用不收集、不分析、不分享任何个人信息，不包含任何统计 SDK、广告 SDK 或第三方追踪组件。

二、权限使用说明

为实现相应功能，本应用会申请以下系统权限，所有处理均在您的设备本地完成：

  • 相机：用于拍摄照片并添加到日记或宠物头像中。
  • 照片图库：用于从相册选择图片添加到日记或宠物头像中。
  • 定位（使用期间）：用于在您主动点击"获取位置"时，为日记附加当前地点名称。位置信息仅保存在本地日记中，不会被上传或持续追踪。
  • 通知：用于按照您创建的提醒时间发送本地通知。通知由系统调度，不经过任何服务器。

以上权限均可随时在系统"设置"中关闭，关闭后仅影响对应功能，不影响其他功能使用。

三、数据存储与删除

  • 数据以本地文件形式存储在应用沙盒内（Documents 目录），并受 iOS 系统级保护。
  • 您可以在应用内"我的 → 清空数据"中删除全部数据。
  • 卸载本应用将同时删除设备上的所有应用数据，删除后无法恢复。

四、儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的任何个人信息。

五、政策变更

如本政策发生变更，我们会在本页面更新并修改顶部"最近更新"日期。重大变更时，我们会在应用内提供显著提示。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

PetDiary (the "App") is an iOS application that helps you record your pets' daily life. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Core Principle: Your Data Belongs to You

  • The App has no servers, no accounts, and makes no network requests.
  • Everything you create (pet profiles, diaries, photos, vaccination, weight, expense, and reminder records) is stored only in the local storage of your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, and we have no access to it.
  • The App does not collect, analyze, or share any personal information. It contains no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking components.

2. Permissions

To provide certain features, the App requests the following system permissions. All processing happens locally on your device:

  • Camera: to take photos for your diary entries or pet avatars.
  • Photo Library: to pick images from your photo library for diary entries or pet avatars.
  • Location (While Using): to attach the current place name to a diary entry only when you explicitly tap "Get Location". Location data is stored locally in the diary entry and is never uploaded or continuously tracked.
  • Notifications: to deliver local notifications at the times you set for reminders. Notifications are scheduled by the operating system and never pass through any server.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in the system Settings. Revoking a permission only affects the corresponding feature.

3. Data Storage and Deletion

  • Data is stored as local files inside the App's sandbox (Documents directory) and is protected by iOS system-level safeguards.
  • You can delete all data at any time via "Profile → Clear All Data" in the App.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all of its data from your device. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. For material changes, we will provide a prominent notice within the App.
